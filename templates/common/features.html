TimeSyncPro Features

Powerful tools for efficient time management

Increase Productivity

Streamline your leave management process with TimeSyncPro's comprehensive solution. Our intuitive dashboard brings together requests, entitlements, and absences in one place, accessible from both desktop and mobile devices.

  • Swift Approval Process: Set notice periods, cancellation approvals, and restrictions to make leave management more effective. Approving leave requests takes moments, not hours, freeing up valuable time for more important tasks.
  • Integrated Solution: Combine holiday planning, absence management, overtime tracking, and employee records in a single platform. This holistic approach ensures you have all the data you need at your fingertips.
  • Flexible Booking System: Customize leave types and absence categories to fit your organization's unique needs. Whether you need to track holidays, sick leave, or even work-from-home days, TimeSyncPro adapts to your requirements.
  • Continuous Improvement: Benefit from regular updates and the ability to suggest new features. We're constantly working to enhance TimeSyncPro based on user feedback and evolving business needs.

TimeSyncPro is designed to work seamlessly across different territories and time zones, making it the perfect solution whether you're managing summer holidays, UK holidays, or leave across global offices.

Tailor the Experience

TimeSyncPro offers unparalleled flexibility to customize your leave management experience. Our system adapts to your organizational structure and workflow preferences, ensuring a perfect fit for your business needs.

  • Organizational Overview: Conveniently view all employees in one list, or filter by team, country, or any custom criteria. Employee information such as role, workgroup, leave year, employment dates, and Bradford Factor are all easily accessible.
  • Customized Workflows: Create Workgroups to organize employees by team, country, office, or any other combination. These Workgroups provide a faster way to view and manage exactly the right people.
  • Flexible Approval Process: Set up a separate configuration for every event type, allowing you the flexibility to have different sets of approvers. You can have two different approvers at the first level and optionally a secondary approver, ensuring thorough oversight of leave requests.
  • Adaptable Setup: As your organization evolves, TimeSyncPro evolves with you. Easily edit your setup to accommodate changes in your company structure or policies.
  • Flexible Time Booking: With the increasing prevalence of flexi-hours and zero-hour contracts, TimeSyncPro provides a robust solution for booking holidays in both days and hours, adapting to modern work arrangements.

Our intuitive UI ensures that no unapproved employees are overlooked, and you can quickly make changes as needed. The system uses your configuration to send requests to managers via email and smartphone apps, with daily reminders to ensure timely action on requests.

Stay in Control

TimeSyncPro puts you firmly in control of your organization's leave management. With our comprehensive suite of tools, you can ensure that all processes and policies are followed consistently across your entire workforce.

  • Universal Access: Your data is available at all times, from anywhere. Access details from any computer at work or home, or even on the go with our mobile app. This flexibility ensures that you're always in control, regardless of your location.
  • Policy Compliance: Configure important rules and restrictions to streamline your holiday leave planner. Control the number of people who can be off on any given day, specify restrictions per workgroup or custom group, and apply these rules for specific periods or generally.
  • Customizable Visibility: Control the visibility of events based on user roles. For instance, you can hide the details of sensitive events like compassionate leave from general employees while allowing line managers to see them.
  • Advanced Leave Rules: Set specific rules such as preventing certain people from being off simultaneously, requiring events to be requested in advance, or allowing only the accrued portion of allowance to be booked. These powerful functions give you complete control over your staff holiday planner.
  • Flexible Handling: Easily handle even the most complicated scenarios, including a variety of work patterns, different leave years, restricted periods, overtime, time off in lieu (TOIL), and several carryover strategies.

TimeSyncPro's control features ensure that your leave management processes are efficient, fair, and align perfectly with your organization's policies and needs.

Easy Automation

TimeSyncPro's automation features streamline your leave management processes, saving time and reducing administrative burdens. By automating routine tasks, you can focus on more strategic aspects of your business.

  • Self-Service Leave Management: Employees can easily request leave and check their allowances via our user-friendly app. Set up one or two levels of approvals for every leave type, and let TimeSyncPro monitor and apply the appropriate policies you've defined.
  • Automated Notifications: Our system uses your desired configuration to send requests to managers via email and app. It can also send daily reminders to ensure that requests are actioned promptly, avoiding approval delays and improving employee morale.
  • Public Holiday Integration: TimeSyncPro automatically allocates local public holidays, taking into account whether they're included in employees' allowances and considering specific employee work patterns.
  • Time-Saving Processes: Save many hours per employee per year by implementing the right automated processes. This efficiency allows you and your team to focus on running your business rather than managing leave requests.
  • Customizable Workflows: Set up approval processes that match your organizational structure. Whether you need simple one-step approvals or complex multi-level workflows, TimeSyncPro adapts to your needs.

With TimeSyncPro's easy automation, you can significantly reduce the time spent on leave management tasks, improve accuracy, and ensure a consistent approach across your organization.
